Crystal Collecter

Directions
There are four crystals displayed at the bottom of the page. At the beginning of the game each crystal is given a different value. The players objective is to match "Your Score" to the "Target Score" by clicking the crystals. The round will end when you match "Your Score" to the "Target Score", or "Your Score" surpasses the "Target Score".
